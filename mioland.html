Self-proclaimed "Saviours of the Annite" burn down a museum

Today, on the 25th of Tiltember, the Museum of Sculptures in the Miolandi capital, Anidh, was burnt down by the extremist group "Saviours of the Annite", also known as ASBOP. Among the lost sculptures was one of the most important Miolandi pieces of art, "The Lady Of The Land" by T. Saini.

A victory for our science

This year's International Physics Prize goes to the Anidh University team for assisting in research of hepta-pionic amplitudes. The Miolandi Journal hereby congratulates these young physicists and wishes them much luck in future research.
